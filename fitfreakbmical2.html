Understanding Your BMI

Your Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or greater

Diet Recommendations

Underweight

High-calorie foods, protein-rich meals, whole milk, nuts, and seeds.

Normal Weight

Balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and low-fat dairy.

Overweight

Low-calorie foods, fiber-rich meals, plenty of fruits and vegetables, lean proteins.

Obesity

Low-calorie foods, high-fiber diet, whole grains, lean protein, avoid sugary drinks and snacks.

Exercise Tips

  • Regular cardiovascular exercise (e.g., walking, running, cycling)
  • Strength training exercises (e.g., weight lifting, resistance bands)
  • Flexibility exercises (e.g., yoga, stretching)
  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)
  • Consistency and gradual progression

Healthy Habits

  • Drink plenty of water
  • Get enough sleep
  • Manage stress through mindfulness or meditation
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption
  • Regular health check-ups
